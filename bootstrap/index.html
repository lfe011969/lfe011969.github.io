Power Apps

In today's fast-paced business landscape, staying competitive means finding efficient solutions to complex challenges. That's where PowerApps comes in. With its powerful and robust capabilities, I've been able to create custom applications tailored to our unique business needs. These apps have streamlined processes, improved productivity, and enhanced decision-making.

This app is used to collect data on students who fail a class. The data is uploaded as an item in a SharePoint list which then triggers the creation of an official Word Document containing the data. This document is uploaded to the SharePoint site and also emailed to the instructor and the academic manager.

Power Automate

Power Automate is the linchpin that ties Microsoft's other Power Platform apps together into a powerful digital toolset. I use Power Automate to transfer critical data between systems, automate complicated and time-consuming tasks, and quickly provide important information to the appropriate decision-makers.

This flow is used to automate the creation of the CSV file necessary for enrolling all students into their perspective classes for a given term. I email the schedule (in Excel format) to a specific address with a specific subject which triggers this flow. This process is in two parts: first, the email attachment is saved to OneDrive and a template file in OneDrive is cleared of all data. In the second step, the data is analyzed using complex logic to determine what classes each student needs to be enrolled in, and the template file is populated with the correct data. At the end of the second step, the CSV file is emailed to myself and a copy is stored on OneDrive.

Power BI

Today's business landscape is shaped from the analyzation of data from a multitude of sources and Power BI provides all the necessary visualizations to quickly gauge important areas. I use Power BI with PowerApps to provide dashboards containing real-time data as well as long-term trends for the appropriate analysis.

This dashboard built in PowerApps is used for monitoring current grade data. The data is automatically exported from the LMS and then imported into SharePoint. The data is refreshed once every hour during the workday which updates the graphics in Power BI.
